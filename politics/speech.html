Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Governor Peter Mbah's speeches focus on the unprecedented economic transformation, security stabilization, and infrastructure development of Enugu State. His primary addresses and key speaking points encompass the following strategic pillars: Economic Growth, Security and Stability, Infrastructure Development, Education and Human Capital Development, Healthcare and Social Welfare, and Governance and Transparency. Through these speeches, Governor Mbah communicates his vision for a prosperous and secure Enugu State, highlighting the achievements of his administration and outlining future initiatives to sustain progress and improve the quality of life for all citizens.
