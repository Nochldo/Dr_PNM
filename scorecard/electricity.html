Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor is executing a sweeping strategy to achieve energy self-sufficiency and exit the national grid by generating 1,000 megawatts of electricity through the state's coal and gas resources. The administration has localized the power sector to drive industrialization and grow the state's economy, with a focus on leveraging Enugu's abundant coal reserves and natural gas to fuel power generation. This ambitious initiative aims to provide reliable and affordable electricity to residents and businesses, reduce dependence on the national grid, and position Enugu as a leader in energy production in Nigeria. By harnessing local resources, Governor Mbah is not only addressing energy challenges but also creating jobs and stimulating economic growth in the state.
