Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor Peter Mbah achieved an Internally Generated Revenue (IGR) of ₦406.77 billion for the 2025 fiscal year, driven largely by non-tax revenue and asset optimization. For the 2026 fiscal year, his administration has set an ambitious IGR target of ₦870 billion to aggressively scale up state infrastructure development and public service delivery, demonstrating a strong commitment to fiscal responsibility and economic growth in Enugu State. And this can be achieved with the massive investments in the tourism and hospitality sector, which is expected to attract over 3 million visitors annually, transforming the state into the premier conference and leisure destination in Africa.
