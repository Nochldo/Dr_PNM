Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor has prioritized healthcare as a core pillar of his administration, committing over half of the state's budget to the health and education sectors. His signature initiatives are designed to eliminate medical tourism and ensure 24-hour access to quality health services across both urban and rural communities. By investing in state-of-the-art medical facilities, training healthcare professionals, and implementing comprehensive health programs, Governor Mbah aims to improve health outcomes, reduce preventable diseases, and enhance the overall well-being of Enugu State residents. These efforts reflect his dedication to building a robust healthcare system that meets the needs of all citizens.
