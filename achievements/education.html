Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor is Governor Peter Mbah of Enugu State is spearheading a massive educational transformation anchored by a record-breaking budget allocation, exceeding 30% of the state's finances. The administration is building 260 "Smart Green Schools" (one in each electoral ward) designed to shift students from rote memorization to experiential, technology-driven learning. These schools are equipped with modern facilities, including digital classrooms, science and technology labs, and renewable energy sources, fostering an environment that nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and innovation. By prioritizing education and investing in the future of Enugu's youth, Governor Mbah aims to cultivate a generation of skilled individuals capable of driving sustainable development and economic growth in the state.
