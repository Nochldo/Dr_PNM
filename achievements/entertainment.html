Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor is spearheading a massive revitalization of the creative and entertainment sector to make Enugu the premier destination for tourism and Nollywood in Africa. Key initiatives include the "042 Arena and Entertainment Park", hosting major events like NAFEST, and winning the prestigious AFRIFF Excellence Award for his efforts in promoting the creative industry. These efforts are expected to attract over 3 million visitors annually, creating thousands of jobs and boosting local businesses, while showcasing Enugu's rich cultural heritage and natural beauty to the world.
