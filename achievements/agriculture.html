Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor Governor Peter Mbah of Enugu State is driving an ambitious agricultural transformation agenda aimed at scaling the state's economy from $4.4 billion to $30 billion and eliminating poverty. His administration focuses on food security, rural economic empowerment, and agribusiness development. Key initiatives include the Enugu State Agricultural Development Program (ESADP), which provides training, access to finance, and market linkages for farmers. The government is also investing in agricultural infrastructure, promoting mechanization, and supporting value chain development to boost productivity and create jobs. By leveraging Enugu's agricultural potential, Governor Mbah aims to enhance food production, increase farmers' incomes, and position the state as a leading agricultural hub in Nigeria.
