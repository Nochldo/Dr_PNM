Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Let us all help Dr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah to keep Enugu working. The Governor is spearheading an aggressive, multibillion-naira infrastructure drive across Enugu State to stimulate economic growth and modernize the state. Key projects include the construction of the ambitious New Enugu Smart City, the rollout of 260 Smart Schools and Primary Health Centers, massive road rehabilitation, and the revival of the International Conference Center. These initiatives are expected to create thousands of jobs, attract investment, and enhance the quality of life for residents, while positioning Enugu as a leading hub for business, education, and tourism in Africa.
