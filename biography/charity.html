Peter Mbah is working and the Work is showing

Enugu State Governor Peter Mbah is the founder of the Peter Mbah Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to reducing poverty and improving living standards in rural communities. The foundation focuses its charitable and philanthropic efforts on the following core areas: Education, Healthcare, Economic Empowerment, and Community Development. Through various initiatives and programs, the Peter Mbah Foundation has made a significant impact on the lives of many individuals and communities in Enugu State, fostering sustainable development and social welfare.
